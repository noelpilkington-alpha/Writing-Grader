AlphaWrite G7 Expository Essay Rubric (0–2 Scale)
Topic Sentences
2 = All body paragraphs start with clear, relevant topic sentences tied to the thesis (each names a distinct way determination or technology broke barriers).
1 = Some topic sentences weak/unclear/missing.
0 = Topic sentences missing or off-topic.
CCSS: W.7.2.A, W.7.2.C

Supporting Details
2 = Each body paragraph includes 3+ well-sequenced, relevant supporting details from the passage.
1 = Some details weak, insufficient, or loosely sequenced.
0 = Details missing/irrelevant/incorrect.
CCSS: W.7.2.B, W.7.2.E, W.7.9

Thesis Statement
2 = Clear, focused thesis explaining how determination and technology can break barriers (sets up distinct subtopics).
1 = Thesis vague/partial.
0 = Missing or off-topic.
CCSS: W.7.2.A, W.7.4

Paragraphs Support the Thesis
2 = Each paragraph develops a distinct, relevant idea (e.g., Michaela's personal determination, Blue Origin's technology adaptations, broader impact on space accessibility for people with disabilities).
1 = Some drift, overlap, or repetition.
0 = Weak or unclear link to thesis.
CCSS: W.7.2.C, W.7.2.E, W.7.9

Sentence Strategies
2 = Varied and correct sentence strategies (complex/compound structures, expansions, appositives) that improve clarity.
1 = Some variety attempted.
0 = Mostly repetitive/simple.
CCSS: W.7.2.D, L.7.3.A, L.7.1.F

Transitions
2 = Logical, varied transitions showing relationships (sequence, cause/effect, examples).
1 = Some transitions but basic/inconsistent.
0 = Missing or confusing transitions.
CCSS: W.7.2.C, W.7.2.E, L.7.3.A

Conventions
2 = Errors rare and do not disrupt meaning.
1 = Some errors but meaning remains clear.
0 = Frequent errors interfere with readability.
CCSS: L.7.1, L.7.2, W.7.5

Introduction & Conclusion
2 = Both ≥ 3 sentences; intro leads to thesis; conclusion restates thesis freshly and ends strongly.
1 = One is underdeveloped.
0 = Weak or missing intro/conclusion.
CCSS: W.7.2.A, W.7.2.F, W.7.4

Evidence Use
2 = Evidence is relevant, well-integrated, and clearly explained (not just listed).
1 = Some evidence but thin/loosely connected/under-explained.
0 = Little/no evidence or inaccurate.
CCSS: W.7.2.B, W.7.2.E, W.7.9

Minimum Paragraph Count
2 = At least 5 full paragraphs (intro, 3 body, conclusion).
1 = 3–4 paragraphs with partial development.
0 = Fewer than 3 paragraphs or incomplete.
CCSS: W.7.2.A–F, W.7.4
